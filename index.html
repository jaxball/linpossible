About Me


I am a computer enthusiast who goes to USC. Born in Lethbridge, Canada, I was raised for the most part in Hong Kong and currently live in Los Angeles. My budding interest in technology has started since elementary school where I got MSN up and running on a NDS with R4DS.

As a maker, I believe the best way to improve our world is to add value by innovation and leadership. Having launched two startups and worked in companies like IBM, I see the key to success in self-motivation. It takes only a heart to outweigh a pack of wolves. Here's to the brave tirelessly solving everyday problems in our lives.

"You don't just do a startup. You do it because you want to solve a problem so bad."
